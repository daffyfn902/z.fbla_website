Scholarship Fund of Alexandria

Frequently Asked Questions:

For Applicants:

How do I apply?

If you are a first time here at Alexandria city Scholarship Fund, you will need to sign up under the ‘new applicant’ form found on the homepage. If you need assistance, please head to the College and Career Center, in room A-121, in the Alexandria City High School - King Street Campus. You can also contact us here.

What types of scholarships are available?

At Alexandria City Scholarship Fund, we offer a diverse range of scholarships designed to cater to the unique aspirations and achievements of high school students. Our scholarship programs encompass various fields of study, including STEM, arts, humanities, and more. We recognize excellence in academics, leadership, community service, and individual passions. Whether you're pursuing a career in science, the arts, or any other field, our goal is to provide opportunities that align with your goals and contribute to your educational journey. From need-based assistance to merit scholarships, we are committed to supporting a wide spectrum of talented and motivated students on their path to success.

Who is eligible for scholarships?

Alexandria City Scholarship Fund’s assistance is only offered to those who attend Alexandria City High School- Minnie Howard Campus, King Street Campus, Satellite Campus, and Chance for Change Campus. To find out if you are eligible for our scholarships, please take our ‘new applicant’ form found on the homepage. After this form is filled out and submitted, speak to a College and Career Center Advisor located in Room A-121 in Alexandria City High School - King Street Campus to learn more about your Scholarship eligibility.

What is the application deadline?

For first time applicants, the form ‘new applicant’ will be opened November 1st and is due no later than January 31th of the school year. For renewal applicants, the ‘renewal application’ will be opened May 1st and is due no later than August 24th at the start of the school year.

For Donors:

How can I contribute to the scholarship fund?

At Alexandria City Scholarship Fund, , contributing to our mission of empowering students is easy and impactful. There are several ways you can make a meaningful contribution. You can make a secure online donation through our website, providing a convenient and transparent way to support deserving students. Additionally, explore employer matching programs to maximize the impact of your contribution. We also welcome inquiries about establishing named scholarships or collaborating on special initiatives. Your generosity directly fuels the dreams of aspiring students, breaking down financial barriers and paving the way for a brighter future. Join us in making a lasting difference in the lives of these promising individuals by contributing to the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria today.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes, donations to the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria are generally tax-deductible. We are a registered nonprofit organization with tax-exempt status, which means that your contribution qualifies as a charitable deduction under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Upon making a donation, you will receive a confirmation receipt or acknowledgment that can be used for tax purposes. We encourage you to consult with a tax professional or refer to applicable tax regulations to ensure compliance with your specific tax situation. Thank you for considering the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria in your philanthropic efforts, and we appreciate your commitment to supporting education and the aspirations of deserving students.

Can I designate my donation for a specific scholarship?

At Alexandria City Scholarship Fund,, we appreciate your enthusiasm for supporting specific scholarships; however, we do not allow donors to designate their contributions to individual scholarships. Our commitment is to fairness, equity, and providing equal opportunities to all eligible students. Designating funds to specific scholarships could create disparities among applicants and compromise the integrity of our selection process. Rest assured that your donation, regardless of the amount, plays a vital role in collectively supporting a diverse group of talented students and making a significant impact on their educational journeys. For more information, please contact us. We thank you for your understanding and for joining us in fostering equal opportunities for all deserving individuals.

What impact will my donation have?

Your donation to the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria will have a profound and lasting impact on the lives of aspiring students. By contributing to our scholarship fund, you are directly empowering individuals to pursue their educational dreams and achieve their full potential. Your support helps alleviate financial barriers, enabling students to access quality education, explore their passions, and contribute meaningfully to society. Scholarships funded by donors like you have a ripple effect, creating a positive and transformative impact not only on the recipients but also on their families and communities. Your generosity is a catalyst for change, fostering a brighter future by investing in the education of promising individuals. We invite you to be a part of this journey and witness the tangible and inspirational outcomes that your donation can bring to the lives of deserving students. Thank you for making a difference with the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria.
