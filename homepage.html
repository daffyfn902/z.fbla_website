Scholarship Fund of Alexandria

Empowering Futures through the Gift of Education

What do we do?

Here at the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria, we provide students with financial assitance to pursue their dreams in higher education.

We believe every student deserves the opportunity to pursue their dreams through accessible education, and we are committed to empowering every student to achieve their full potential.

Why should you contribute?

At the Scholarship Fund of Alexandria, we're dedicated to ensuring every student has the chance for higher education. With the average four-year degree costing $140,000, many students, especially the 45% at Alexandria City High School on free or reduced lunch, face significant financial hurdles. Your support provides resources to overcome these constraints, empowering students to achieve their academic dreams and fostering a more inclusive and equitable society.

How can you help the movement?

If you want to support our cause, the best way is to donate here. Every dollar counts, and your contribution is greatly appreciated.
